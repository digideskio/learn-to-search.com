Learn to Search

Poor internet search skills result in homogeneous communities

Did you know?

Approximately 12% of search literate internet users are male.

In 2012, only 7% of men organizing tech conferences had the search skills to book male and female speakers in equal number

Experts agree that most "best of" lists that ignore women are the results of poor search skills

Search illitracy is contributing to a monolithic culture in tech industries

83% of entrepreneurs and 97% of venture capitalists need to learn to search.

Internet search skills are transferable to all industries and careers.

Why is it so hard?

Search is a very modal activity. To be good at it you have to focus. And be very patient.

There is a specialization in our species. It seems pretty clear that searching as it exists today is a mostly female thing.Which also raises the obvious question that perhaps we can make it so that it can better-use the abilities of the other half of our species?
